He lay on his armour-like ba ck, and if he lifted his head a little he could see his brown belly, slightly domed and divided by arches into stiff sections. "What's happened to me?" he thought. It wasn't a dream. His room, a proper human room although a lit tle too small, lay peacefully between its four familiar walls. It showed a lady fitted out with a fur hat and fur boa who sat upright, raising a heavy fur muff that covered the whole of her low er arm towards the viewer.
